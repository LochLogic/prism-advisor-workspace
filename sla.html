Service Level Agreement

Effective 4 June 2026 · Last updated 4 June 2026

This SLA is incorporated into and forms a part of the Prism Advisor Workspace Terms of Service between LeMay Ventures LLC ("Operator", "we", or "us") and the Customer ("you"). It governs the availability and uptime of the Prism Advisor Workspace service.

1. Definitions

  • "Service" means the Prism Advisor Workspace cloud-based platform.
  • "Downtime" means the Service is completely inaccessible or entirely incapable of performing its primary functions for all users within your workspace, measured from the server-side.
  • "Uptime Percentage" is calculated as the total number of minutes in a calendar month, minus the total number of minutes of Downtime in that month, divided by the total number of minutes in that month.
  • "Scheduled Maintenance" means planned downtime for system upgrades, updates, and maintenance.
  • "Service Credit" means a dollar credit, calculated as a percentage of your monthly subscription fee, that we may credit back to an eligible account.

2. Uptime commitment

We commit to maintaining an Uptime Percentage of at least 99.9% during each calendar month (the "Uptime Commitment"). If we do not meet the Uptime Commitment, you will be eligible to request a Service Credit as described below.

3. Scheduled maintenance

To ensure the security and performance of the Service, we periodically require maintenance windows.

  • We will use commercially reasonable efforts to schedule maintenance during non-peak hours (e.g., weekends or outside standard U.S. market trading hours).
  • We will provide at least 48 hours of advance notice for Scheduled Maintenance via email or in-app notification.
  • Time spent executing Scheduled Maintenance does not count as Downtime.

4. Service credits

If the Uptime Percentage falls below 99.9% in a given calendar month, you are eligible for a Service Credit based on the following tiers:

Uptime PercentageService Credit
Less than 99.9% but ≥ 98.0%10% of the applicable monthly fee
Less than 98.0% but ≥ 95.0%20% of the applicable monthly fee
Less than 95.0%30% of the applicable monthly fee

Limitations on Credits. Service Credits are your sole and exclusive remedy for any performance or availability issues. Service Credits may not be exchanged for cash, cannot exceed 30% of your total monthly bill, and will only be applied to future subscription charges.

5. Exclusions

The Uptime Commitment does not apply to any Downtime, performance issues, or unavailability resulting from:

  • Third-Party Services: Outages or disruptions caused by third-party infrastructure or integration partners (including, but not limited to, Supabase, Cloudflare, Stripe, Plaid, and Google).
  • Force Majeure: Events outside our reasonable control, including natural disasters, acts of war, terrorism, government action, or widespread internet or telecommunications failures.
  • Customer Actions: Your acts or omissions, including misconfiguration of the Service, failure to follow our documented instructions, or use of the Service in violation of the Terms or Acceptable Use Policy.
  • Suspension: Downtime resulting from our suspension or termination of your account in accordance with the Terms (e.g., for non-payment or security threats).
  • Beta Features: Any features, services, or environments designated as "beta," "preview," or "early access."

6. Claiming a service credit

To receive a Service Credit, you must submit a claim by emailing support@prismaw.com within thirty (30) days following the end of the month in which the Downtime occurred. Your claim must include:

  • "SLA Claim" in the subject line.
  • The dates, times, and duration of the claimed Downtime.
  • Any relevant logs or documentation corroborating the outage.

We will evaluate all information reasonably available to us and make a good faith determination of whether a Service Credit is owed.
