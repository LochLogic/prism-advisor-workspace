## Pre-post checklist (read before every post)

- [ ] **Visuals attached** - 2 to 3 screenshots or a short clip of the client roadmap.
      Visual posts strongly outperform text-only. Easy to forget in the moment.
- [ ] Fresh angle - check the "Angles used" table below so we aren't repeating.
- [ ] Soft CTA only - keep the ~3 value posts per 1 ask cadence.
- [ ] Founder reshare - Cory reshares from his personal profile with one personal line.
      That reshare is where most of the reach comes from.
- [ ] No em-dashes (house style).
