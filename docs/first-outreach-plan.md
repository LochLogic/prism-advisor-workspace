# First Design-Partner Outreach — Action Plan

A concrete 3-week sprint to land your **first 3–5 design partners**, built on `docs/design-partner-kit.md` (ICP, offer, channels, templates). The kit is the *what to say*; this is the *what to do, when*.

**Goal:** 3–5 solo/small fee-only RIAs onboarded as design partners in ~3 weeks.
**One success metric to track:** # of partners who've (a) had a household imported and (b) logged in twice.

---

## Gate — do NOT start outreach until these are true
You're inviting fiduciaries to look at a tool that will hold client data. Before the first message:
- [ ] **Inboxes live** (`privacy@`/`legal@`/`security@` + a real `cory@` or `hello@prismaw.com` reply address).
- [ ] **Supabase Pro** (so "where's my data / backups?" has a real answer) — Checklist 1.1.
- [ ] **You've walked the demo end-to-end yourself** once, on desktop and phone, so a live screen-share is smooth.
- [ ] A **1-tab tracker** exists (below).

*(Counsel review and live Stripe are NOT gates for design partners — partners are free.)*

---

## The funnel (realistic numbers)
Warm intros convert ~10× better than cold. Weight everything toward warm first.

| Stage | Cold/community | Warm intro |
|---|---|---|
| Touch → reply | ~15% | ~60% |
| Reply → demo call | ~40% | ~70% |
| Demo → "yes, let's do it" | ~35% | ~50% |

**Math to 5 partners:** ~8–12 warm asks **+** ~40–60 cold/community touches. That's the whole sprint.

---

## Week 0 (2–3 evenings) — Build the list + tracker
**Tracker columns** (Google Sheet is fine):
`Name · Firm · Source(warm/XYPN/NAPFA/Kitces/LinkedIn/Reddit) · Channel · Status(todo→contacted→replied→demo'd→onboarding→active→passed) · Last touch · Next action · Notes`

**Fill ~50 rows, in this priority order:**
1. **Warm (target 10–15):** anyone you know who *is* a solo/small RIA, or who *knows* one. Text/DM friends: "Do you know any fee-only financial advisors running their own shop? I'm building something for them." Each "yes" → an intro request.
2. **XYPN members (target 15):** the densest ICP. Find names via the XYPN "Find an Advisor" directory + their community.
3. **NAPFA (target 10):** fee-only, often solo; regional study groups.
4. **LinkedIn (target 10):** search `"fee-only" financial planner` / `RIA founder` / `solo advisor`; prioritize people who *post* (they're reachable + value tools).
5. **Reddit/X (target 5):** r/CFP regulars; #FinTwit fee-only planners.

---

## Week 1 — Warm first, seed the channel
**Daily rhythm (~45 min):**
- **5 warm touches/day** — send the intro-request or, once intro'd, the warm email (kit template). Warm is your highest-yield; do it first and fast.
- **1 founder post** every 2–3 days (use `docs/founder-content-starter.md`). Post #1 (origin) and #2 (between-meetings wedge) this week. This warms cold outreach later ("oh, I saw your post").
- Log every touch in the tracker.

**End-of-week-1 target:** 10–15 warm touches sent, 2–3 demos booked, 2 posts up.

## Week 2 — Community + direct, run the demos
**Daily rhythm (~60 min):**
- **8–10 cold/community touches/day** using the kit's LinkedIn DM / email / community-post templates. **Personalize the first line** every time (reference their firm/post) — generic = ignored.
- **Run booked demos** (see call flow below). Aim for 2–3 demos this week.
- Post #3 (don't-rip-out-your-stack) + #5 (the design-partner ask) — #5 doubles as inbound.

**End-of-week-2 target:** ~40 cold/community touches total, 4–6 demos done, **2–3 partners committed**.

## Week 3 — Convert, onboard, follow up
- **Onboard the committed partners** (white-glove: you import 2–3 real households with them on a call).
- **Follow-up sequence** on non-responders (below).
- Convert 1–2 more from the pipeline → **3–5 partners total**.
- Schedule each partner's first feedback touchpoint.

---

## The message sequence (per prospect)
Spread over ~10 days; stop when they reply.
1. **Day 0** — initial (warm email or cold DM; kit templates). One clear ask: *"worth a 20-min look?"*
2. **Day 3** — soft follow-up: *"Bumping this up — happy to just send the demo link if a call's easier."* + the demo link.
3. **Day 8** — value, no ask: share one relevant founder post or a one-line insight; *"no worries if the timing's off."*
4. Then stop. Mark `passed`. (Re-approach in a quarter if they engaged at all.)

## The demo call flow (20–25 min)
1. **2 min** — their world: *"Tell me how you handle the client-facing side of planning today."* (Listen for the pain; you'll mirror it back.)
2. **8 min** — screen-share the demo, **open on the client roadmap** (the wedge), then the live "discuss with advisor" thread. *Lead with the differentiator, not the admin grid.*
3. **5 min** — show the advisor side: roster, one-click report, the workspace.
4. **3 min** — the offer: free white-glove onboarding + founding pricing, in exchange for honest feedback over ~2 months.
5. **Close:** *"Want to pick one real household and set it up together this week?"* (A concrete next step beats "think about it.")

## Objection handling (RIAs will ask)
- **"Is my client data safe?"** → row-level isolation, MFA, encryption; point to `prismaw.com/security.html` + offer the DPA. *(Honest: don't overclaim 17a-4.)*
- **"Another tool?"** → "It's the client-facing layer on top of your custodian/reporting — not a replacement. The goal is fewer logins, not more."
- **"Who are you / is this a real company?"** → LeMay Ventures LLC; your name + POV are on LinkedIn (this is why the content matters).
- **"I'm too small / too busy."** → "That's exactly who it's for, and white-glove means *I* do the setup. 20 minutes total from you."
- **"What's it cost?"** → "Free for design partners; founding pricing locked in after."

---

## Definition of a successful design partner
- You imported ≥2 real households together.
- They've logged in unprompted at least twice.
- They've given you ≥1 round of candid feedback.
- They'd give you a 1-line testimonial + logo when asked.

## If it stalls (honest checkpoints)
- **No replies after ~30 cold touches?** → the message or the targeting is off, not the volume. Tighten the first line / re-pick the ICP. (Lean harder on warm.)
- **Demos but no yes?** → listen for the *real* objection on the call and address it directly; consider whether the wedge is landing.
- **It's working but slow?** → that's normal for B2B founder outreach. 3–5 partners is the goal, not 50.
