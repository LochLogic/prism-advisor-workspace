Everything an advisor relationship needs

One workspace for planning, communication, workflow, and the records your regulators expect.

Seven-horizon roadmaps

Guide every household from foundation to legacy through a structured, milestone-based lifecycle plan — a living roadmap each client can open any time, with built-in calculators for reserves, debt, asset location, Monte Carlo, Roth ladders, and estate.

A relationship that lives between meetings

Clients flag questions and reply in threaded conversations on any milestone; advisors get live notifications, deep-linked to the exact phase — so the advice keeps moving instead of going quiet between quarterly reviews.

CRM & workflow, where the planning happens

Tasks with due dates and priorities, a book-wide "next actions" queue, client pipeline stages, one-click review cadences, and a complete interaction timeline — all tied to the same client record as the roadmap, so nothing is re-entered.

A compliance-minded foundation

An append-only audit trail, immutable profile versioning, optional two-factor authentication, and a per-client export — built around SEC 17a-3 / 17a-4 record-keeping principles, so a growing practice stays accountable.
